# 🧭 Career Compass
### AI-Powered Career Guidance Platform for Students

A data analytics web application that helps students after 12th or during college
explore careers, analyse skill gaps, compare roles, and get personalised roadmaps
across 7 major career domains.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
Students after 12th face difficulty choosing careers due to:
- Too many options with no structured guidance
- Scattered resources across YouTube, blogs, and courses
- No personalised roadmap based on individual background

---

## ✅ Features
- 🗺️ **Explore Roles** — Browse 35+ careers across 7 domains with full skill roadmaps
- 📊 **Skill Gap Analyser** — See your readiness % and missing skills with priority order
- ⚖️ **Compare Careers** — Side by side salary, demand, time and skill overlap
- 🤖 **AI Roadmap Generator** — Personalised month-by-month career plan
- 📈 **Data Insights** — 6 interactive charts from real career data
